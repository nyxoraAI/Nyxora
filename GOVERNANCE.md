# 🏛️ Nyxora Governance & Foundation Vision

Welcome to the official Governance document for **Nyxora**. 

This document outlines the evolutionary roadmap of Nyxora from a privately maintained open-source AI Agent into a community-governed Web3 ecosystem managed by the **Nyxora Foundation**.

---

## 🌍 The Dual-Entity Vision

To ensure long-term sustainability, legal compliance, and rapid innovation, the Nyxora ecosystem is designed around a dual-entity philosophy (following industry standards set by Optimism and Arbitrum):

### 1. Nyxora Foundation (The Open Source Guardian)
A non-profit entity dedicated to:
- Maintaining the core open-source repositories of the Nyxora Web3 AI Agent.
- Fostering developer education and community growth.
- Managing the ecosystem **Treasury** and distributing Grants to developers building plugins, MCP servers, and integrations for Nyxora.
- Ensuring the protocol remains decentralized, agnostic, and unbiased.

### 2. Nyxora Labs (The Commercial Engine)
An independent software development organization focused on:
- Building enterprise-grade, commercial products on top of the Nyxora framework.
- Providing managed VPS hosting, premium endpoints, and SLA-backed support for institutional users.

---

## ⚖️ Governance Model & Decision Making

### Current Stage: Core Team Consensus
Currently, Nyxora operates under a **Core Team Consensus** model. Major architectural decisions (such as integrating new bridges, modifying the cryptographic signer, or adopting new MCP protocols) are decided by the core maintainers based on community feedback via GitHub Issues and Pull Requests.

### Future Stage: Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO)
As the project matures, governance will transition to a **DAO structure**. 
Key protocol upgrades, treasury allocation, and ecosystem directions will be proposed and voted upon transparently using on-chain governance mechanisms (e.g., Snapshot, Tally).

We believe that code is law, and contributions are the lifeblood of the Foundation.
